Cookie Recipes

Almond Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 115g of Softeneed Butter
  • 1/2 Cup of Brown Sugar
  • 1 Egg
  • 2 Teaspoons of Almond Essence
  • 1/2 Cup of Almond Meal
  • 1 1/4 Cups of Flour
  • (Optional) 20 Almonds for decoration
  1. Combine the butter and sugar in a medium bowl. One at a time, beat in eggs, almond essence and almon meal.
  2. Fold the flour into the mixutre until its smooth.
  3. Preheat the oven to 200°C.
  4. Roll 1 inch balls of the mixture on ungreased baking trays, pressing an almond on top of each cookie.
  5. Bake for approximately 5 to 8 minutes or until the cookies become light in color..

Crinkle Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 Cup of Cocoa Powder
  • 2 Cups of Brown Sugar
  • 1 Cup of Flour
  • 1/2 a Cup of Canola Oil
  • 4 Eggs
  • 2 Teaspoons of Baking Powder
  • 2 Teaspoons of Vanilla Extract
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of Salt
  • 1 Cup of Icing Sugar OR Confectioners Sugar
  1. Combine the cocoa powder, sugar and vegetable into one large bowl. Gradually beat in the eggs, adding the vanila extract after completely mixed.
  2. In a seperate medium bowl, mix the flour, baking powder and salt.
  3. Fold the flour mix into the large bowl until smooth.
  4. Allow to chill for 3 to 5 hours.
  5. Preheat the oven to 175°C.
  6. Roll the mix as 1 inch balls, in the icing sugar before placing it onto a tray with baking paper.
  7. Bake for approximately 10 to 12 minutes.
  8. Note: The cookies keep baking after you take it out of the oven, so give it time to cool down!
