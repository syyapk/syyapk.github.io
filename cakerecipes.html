Oreo Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 350g of Oreo Cookies
  • 80g of Butter
  • 375g of Softened Philadelphia Block Cream
    Cheese
  • 1/2 Cup of Caster Sugar
  • 1 Teaspoon of Vanilla
  • 1 Cup of Cream
  • 3 Teaspoons of Powdered Gelatine (Dissolved in 1/4 Cup of Boiling water)
  • 200g of Melted White Chocolate
  • (Optional) Strawberries for decoration
  • (Optional) Extra Oreo Cookies for decoration
  1. Compress the Oreo cookies into little crumbs, combining with the melted butter when completely flattened.
  2. Press the mixture into a lined 18cm to 28cm lamington tin and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  3. Beat the Philedelphia block cream cheese, and the vanilla until it is smooth (You can use a mixer or do it manually). Slowly combine the cream with this mixture.
  4. Stir through the gelatine mixture and white chocolate, afterwards cutting the remaining Oreo cookies into little bits. Pour them all into the same batter.
  5. Poor onto the Oreo base and then refrigerate for 3 to 5 hours.
