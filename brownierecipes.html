Brownie Recipes

Chocolate fudge brownies

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 Cup of Butter
  • 228g of Quality, Sweet Chocolate
  • 3/4 Cup of White Sugar
  • 1/4 Cup of Brown Sugar
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 Teaspoon of Vanilla Extract
  • 1/2 Cup and 2 Tablespoons of Flour
  • 2 Tablespoons of Cocoa Powder
  • 1/4 Salt
  • 1 Cup of Chocolate Chips
  1. Allow the butter and sweet chocolate (chopped into little pieces) to sit on a pan in medium heat, stirring regularly until they are completely melted. Pour into a bowl to allow it to cool.
  2. Preheat the oven to 175Â°C.
  3. Mix both white and brown sugar into the bowl with the melted chocolate/butter. One at a time, add the eggs and the vanilla extract into the mixture.
  4. Combine the cocoa powder, salt and the flour into the chocolate bowl. Once it is completely mixed together, add the chocolate chips into the mixture.
  5. Pour the finished batter into the pan and set it in the oven for approximately 35 minutes.
  6. Note: Be sure to keep an eye on your brownies!
